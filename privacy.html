Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026
RepRoute is a field sales intelligence tool for outside sales representatives. We collect only what's necessary to make the app work. We do not sell your data. We do not run ads.

1. Who We Are

RepRoute ("we," "our," or "the app") is a web-based field sales application available at reproute.tech. It is operated as a business productivity tool for sales teams in the construction and equipment rental industry.

2. Information We Collect

Account information
When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. We use this to identify your account and display your name in the app. We do not receive your Google password.

Location data (GPS)
The app uses your device's GPS to verify check-ins at customer sites. Location is only accessed when you actively tap the Check-In button. We record the GPS coordinates at the time of check-in, the distance from the customer's pinned location, and GPS accuracy. We do not track your location continuously or in the background.

Business data you enter
This includes companies, prospects, bid projects, visit notes, follow-up reminders, route plans, and daily reports that you create in the app. This data is stored in your account and is only accessible to you and, where applicable, your manager.

Google Calendar data
If you connect Google Calendar, the app reads your calendar events to display them in the Activity Log. We read event titles and descriptions only. We do not modify, delete, or write to your calendar.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and keep your account secure
  • To display your territory data, prospects, and bids in the app
  • To verify GPS check-ins at customer locations
  • To generate mileage reports and daily activity summaries
  • To share activity reports with your manager when you choose to submit them
  • To calculate route distances and prospect scores

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Your data may be visible to:

  • Your manager — if your account is assigned a manager role in the same organization, they can view your check-in history, submitted reports, and leaderboard stats
  • Supabase — our database provider, which stores your data on secure servers. Supabase is SOC 2 compliant. See supabase.com/privacy
  • Vercel — our hosting provider. See vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in a secured Supabase database with row-level security (RLS) enabled. This means each user can only access their own data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled entirely by Google OAuth.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below and we will permanently remove it within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for Google Calendar access at any time via your Google Account settings

8. Children's Privacy

RepRoute is intended for use by adults in a professional context. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
