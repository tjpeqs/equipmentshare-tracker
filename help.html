Getting Started
FIRST TIME SETUP
01
1
Sign in with Google

Go to reproute.tech and tap "Sign in with Google." Use your work Gmail. Access is restricted to authorized team members only.

2
Complete the Setup Wizard

First-time users see a quick 4-step setup: enter your name, territory, role (Rep or Manager), and name your route days. Takes under 60 seconds.

3
Add to Home Screen

On iPhone: tap Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android: tap the browser menu → Add to Home Screen. RepRoute works like a native app — no App Store needed.

4
Customize Your Routes

Tap ⚙ ROUTES in the top right to rename each day's route to match your territory — e.g. "Monday: Torrington Loop."

💡
RepRoute works offline. If you lose signal in the field, check-ins and actions are saved locally and sync automatically when you reconnect.

Territory Map
YOUR PINNED PROSPECTS ON A MAP
03
📍
Color-coded pins

Each company is pinned with a color matching its route day — blue (MON), purple (TUE), green (WED), yellow (THU), red (FRI). Filter by day to see just your route for the day.

➕
Add a company

Tap "+ Add Company," fill in the name, town, type, phone, and route day. Coordinates auto-fill from the town name — or enter lat/lng manually for a precise pin.

⏰
Set a follow-up reminder

Tap the ⏰ button on any company to set a reminder date. Presets: Tomorrow, 3 Days, 1 Week, 2 Weeks, 1 Month. Overdue reminders surface on the Top Prospects screen.

🚗
Waze & Maps links

Select any company in the sidebar to reveal Waze, Google Maps, phone, and email buttons. One tap launches navigation directly to that address.

✎
Edit or remove

Use the ✎ and ✕ buttons next to any company in the sidebar to edit details or remove them from your map.

📍
Find nearby prospects

Tap "📍 NEARBY" in the top nav bar to search for construction companies near your current GPS location. Tap + ADD to instantly pin them to your map.

Check-In
GPS-VERIFIED SITE VISITS
04
1
Find the company

Use the left sidebar to find the company you're visiting. Filter by route day or search by name.

2
Tap CHECK IN

A visit form appears — fill in what happened: contacted decision maker, left materials, quoted equipment, quote value, next step, and notes.

3
GPS verifies your location

Your phone's GPS checks if you're within 1,000 feet of the company's pin. Within range = ✓ Verified (green). Outside range = ⚠ Far (amber). Either way, the visit is logged.

4
View today's visits

The right panel shows all of today's check-ins with time, distance from pin, and status. Companies you've already visited show a blue ✓ badge.

✓ VERIFIED

GPS confirmed you were within 1,000 ft of the pin when you checked in.

⚠ FAR

You were more than 1,000 ft from the pin. Visit is still logged — pin location may just be imprecise.

• LOGGED

Company has no coordinates on file. Visit logged without distance verification.

💡
Tap the History tab inside Check-In to see your full visit log. Filter by date to find any past visit.

Route Planner
CLICK-TO-PLAN DAILY ROUTES
05
1
Click pins to build your route

Click any company pin on the map to add it as a numbered stop. Click again to remove it. Filter by route day to see only that day's companies.

2
Reorder stops

Use the ↑ ↓ arrows in the left sidebar to reorder your stops. A dashed red line connects them on the map so you can see the route visually.

3
Launch navigation

Tap 🚗 Waze or 🗺 Maps to open your full multi-stop route in navigation. All stops are pre-loaded — no re-entering addresses.

4
Save your route

Name the route (e.g. "Monday Run Apr 28") and tap Save. It's stored under Saved Routes — tap LOAD to replay it any day, tap GO to launch navigation immediately.

Activity Log
GOOGLE CALENDAR SYNC
06
🗓
Weekly calendar view

Shows 7 days Mon–Sun. Each day displays your Google Calendar events — company name as the title, visit notes in the description. Use ‹ › arrows to go week by week, or tap TODAY to snap back.

⟳
Sync button

Tap ⟳ SYNC to pull the latest events from Google Calendar. Events update automatically when you change weeks.

📤
Share to Manager

Each day with events shows a SHARE button. Tap it to submit that day's activity snapshot to your manager. Once shared, it turns green with a ✓.

📋
Submitted reports

The right sidebar shows all reports you've shared. Managers see every rep's submissions with a rep filter dropdown.

💡
Log your field stops in Google Calendar using the company name as the event title and your visit notes in the description. RepRoute pulls both automatically.
🚗
Mileage Tracker
AUTOMATIC EXPENSE TRACKING
07
📏
Auto-calculated miles

Every route you save in Route Planner automatically calculates the total miles driven based on your stops' coordinates. No manual entry needed.

💵
IRS reimbursement

Mileage is automatically multiplied by the current IRS standard rate ($0.67/mile) to show your weekly reimbursement total.

⬇
Export CSV

Tap "Export CSV" to download a spreadsheet of the week's routes, dates, stops, miles, and reimbursement amounts — ready to submit to your manager.

Top Prospects
YOUR DAILY CALL LIST, RANKED
08

Tap 🎯 TOP PROSPECTS in the top nav to see your companies ranked by heat score. The algorithm considers:

🔥 Priority

High-priority companies score higher. Set priority when adding or editing a company.

📅 Visit recency

Companies you haven't visited in 30+ days score higher. Companies visited recently score lower.

🏗 Local bids

Companies in towns with active bids in your pipeline score higher — more opportunity nearby.

⚡ Action alerts

Overdue follow-up reminders and high-priority companies not visited in 30+ days show as action items at the top.

💡
Check Top Prospects every morning before you head out. It tells you exactly who to hit first based on what's actually happening in your territory.

Proposal Builder
SEND QUOTES FROM THE FIELD
09
1
Tap 📄 PROPOSAL in the nav bar

Available from any screen — no need to leave what you're doing.

2
Fill in company and contact details

Enter the company name, contact name, and email address.

3
Add equipment items

Select equipment from the dropdown (excavators, skid steers, lifts, generators, etc.), set quantity, rental duration, and daily rate. Add as many items as needed.

Manager Features
TEAM VISIBILITY & REPORTING
10
🏆
Rep Leaderboard

The LEADERBOARD tab (visible to managers) ranks all reps by check-ins, GPS-verified visits, and reports submitted. Filter by 7-day, 30-day, or all-time. Updates in real time.

📍
Check-In History

In the Check-In tab, the History screen shows every rep's visits. Filter by rep or date. See distance from pin and GPS verification status for each visit.

📤
Submitted Reports

In the Activity Log, the right sidebar shows all reports from every rep. Filter by rep name to review any individual's daily activity.

👤
Role assignment

Reps are set as "rep" role by default. Contact your admin to be assigned the "manager" role, which unlocks the leaderboard and all-rep views.

⚠
Manager views are only visible to accounts with the manager role. If you should have manager access and don't, contact your RepRoute admin.

Tips & Shortcuts
GET THE MOST OUT OF REPROUTE
11
⚙ Customize routes

Tap ⚙ ROUTES in the nav to rename your route days to match your territory. Each rep's names are saved independently.

🌤 Weather check

The weather widget in the nav bar shows current conditions and a green "FIELD OK" or amber "CHECK CONDITIONS" indicator before you head out.

📴 Offline mode

A red OFFLINE banner appears when you lose signal. Check-ins are saved locally and sync automatically when you reconnect.

🔁 Sign out

Tap SIGN OUT in the top right. Your data stays in the database — everything will be there next time you log in.

📱 Add to home screen

For the best experience, add RepRoute to your iPhone or Android home screen. It launches full-screen like a native app.

🔒 Your data is private

Each rep only sees their own companies, bids, and check-ins. No rep can see another's data unless they are a manager.
